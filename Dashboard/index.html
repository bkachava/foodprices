Population

By the year 2050, the world population is expected to grow to 9.7 billion, and México will have 148 million of inhabitants.

Demand for food will grow.

As populations rise, urbanization increases and incomes grow, the agricultural sector will be under mounting pressure to meet the demand for safe and nutritious food.

Agriculture

In 2015, the 83% of arable soil in North America was used for family farming.

By 2050 the value of crops in developing countries is expected to account for an average of 3.5 percent of the output value of all sectors – contrasting with an average of only 0.6 percent in developed economies – due to a combination of their projected path to development and climate change.

Approach
